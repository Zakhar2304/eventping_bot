EventPing

EventPing is a Telegram bot that connects to your Google Calendar so you can manage events and get reminders without leaving Telegram.

Open EventPing in Telegram

Purpose of the application

EventPing helps people plan their day by bridging Telegram and Google Calendar. After you sign in with Google, the bot can:

  • connect securely to your Google Calendar using OAuth 2.0;
  • list upcoming calendar events;
  • create and edit events (title, date, time, description);
  • send Telegram reminders before events, based on your settings.

The official Telegram bot is @eventping_bot.

How it works

  1. Open EventPing in Telegram.
  2. Connect Google Calendar and grant calendar access.
  3. Create events or browse upcoming ones.
  4. Receive reminders in Telegram before each event.

Data & privacy

EventPing only uses Google Calendar data to provide bot features you request. You can disconnect the calendar in the bot and revoke Google access at any time.
